ReelRoom Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

Introduction

ReelRoom is a Chrome extension that enables synchronized video watching experiences across multiple devices. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data We Collect

Authentication Information

  • Google account information (name, email address) for user verification
  • Authentication tokens for maintaining your session

Room and Synchronization Data

  • Room creation and participation information
  • Video synchronization events (play, pause, seek timestamps)
  • Temporary session data for coordinating watch parties

How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain secure sessions
  • Room Management: To create, join, and manage watch party rooms
  • Video Synchronization: To coordinate playback across multiple participants
  • User Experience: To maintain your preferences and session state

Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage APIs
  • Room coordination data is temporarily stored on secure Supabase servers
  • Video synchronization events are transmitted only between active room participants
  • All data transmission uses encrypted HTTPS connections

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Video synchronization data shared only with other participants in your watch party room
  • Authentication handled securely through Google OAuth (no credentials stored on our servers)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

Data Retention

  • Room data is automatically deleted when rooms are closed or become inactive
  • Authentication sessions expire and are renewed automatically
  • No long-term storage of viewing history or personal preferences
  • Local browser data is cleared when you uninstall the extension

Your Rights

  • Revoke access at any time through your Google account settings
  • Clear all local data by uninstalling the extension
  • Leave any room immediately to stop data sharing with other participants
  • Contact us for questions about your data

Streaming Platform Interaction

ReelRoom interacts with video content on supported streaming platforms (Netflix, YouTube, etc.) only to:

  • Detect video playback state for synchronization
  • Execute play/pause commands for coordinated viewing
  • We do not collect, store, or analyze your viewing preferences or history
  • We respect the terms of service of all supported platforms

Children's Privacy

ReelRoom is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Updates will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
